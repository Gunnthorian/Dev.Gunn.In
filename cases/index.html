SFF MK1

This is the MK1, the first SFF PC case I've worked on and designed. Countless hours have been put into designing it and testing it. This case is designed to be versatile, allowing for system watercooling with a radiator 280mm in length and fitting full sized graphics cards.

Specifications

  • Exterior Dimensions: 11.4 x 9.1 x 8.3 (in) 14 Litres
  • Powersupply: fits full sized powersupplies
  • Graphics Card: fits dual slot Founder's Edition 2080s
  • Supports mini-ITX motherboards

Modularity

For an SFF case this one is equipt with a lot of customization, starting with being made from any material and color you choose. A range of CPU coolers can be used, standard sized PSUs, full sized graphics cards, and all with room to spare.

Where to get one?

The MK1 will be opensource for anyone to make, if you don't have access to 3D-printing and laser-cutting then these services can be outsourced.

If you like the end result of the PC and have cash to spare after the build a donation would be much appreciated. Support like this is what makes me want to make the next project bigger and better, in this case smaller and better.
